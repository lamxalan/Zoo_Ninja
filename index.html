Welcome to Zoo-Ninja!

Pick the right animal category and slice with a swipe.

How to Play

  • Each round has a target category (like Birds or Reptiles).
  • Slice animals that belong to the target to earn 10 points.
  • Slice 3 wrong animals in a round and the game ends.
  • Each new round needs more correct slices to win.
  • Use your mouse or touch screen to swipe through animals.
